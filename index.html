Projects

We design, develop, and ship digital products that drive success for our clients through innovative solutions, user-centred design, and cutting-edge technology. Our goal is to deliver impactful solutions that exceed expectations and propel our clients towards their goals.

Online consulting platform

The online counseling platform is a responsive web application. It offers low-threshold chat counseling for people with depression, addiction problems or financial difficulties and allows you to remain anonymous.

Lumen

Lumen is a finance app that integrates all financial instruments of the user in one place. Simplified cash flow management and the ability to send money directly to contacts are the main value propositions of the MVP version.

Relaxify

Relaxify is a scientifically backed mental health app designed to assist individuals in overcoming depression, anxiety, and post-traumatic stress. It offers cognitive exercises and stress-reducing techniques, coupled with regular surveys to assess and track improvements over time.
